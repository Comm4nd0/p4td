About us

We run the kind of day care we'd want for our own dogs.

Paws 4 Thought started in 2016 with a simple idea — that dogs deserve more than a kennel. Our ten-acre site sits in quiet Buckinghamshire countryside, split into safe, supervised groups of dogs with similar temperament and size.

Every dog gets individual attention, loads of stimulation, and comes home tired in the best way. Plus pick-up and drop-off if you need it.

Training

Give your puppy the best possible start.

Our four-week puppy course is run on-site by qualified trainers using positive reinforcement. You'll bond with your dog, they'll socialise with theirs, and you'll both leave with the foundations for a lifetime.

  • Sit, stay, come, loose-lead walking
  • Calm socialisation with other pups

A day here

From drop-off
to tired tails.

Here's roughly how every day plays out. Timings flex with the group, the weather, and whoever's feeling bouncy.

07:00

Arrival
