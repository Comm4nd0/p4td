Day Care

Our dog day care gives your dog the very best day, every day. Set across 10 secure, council-licensed acres, your dog enjoys a full programme of play, rest, and enrichment under the watchful eye of our experienced team.

  • Supervised group play in spacious outdoor fields
  • Positive socialisation with other dogs
  • Feeding as per your instructions
  • Structured rest periods throughout the day
  • Enrichment activities and mental stimulation
  • Updates and photos sent to you during the day
