01 · Service

Day Care

A ten-acre site of secure countryside, grouped by temperament, supervised all day.

Every dog is unique, and every day at Paws 4 Thought reflects that. Our outdoor site covers ten acres of fenced land split into multiple fields, so dogs are grouped by size, age, and play style — not just thrown together.

Our staff are trained in canine body language and first aid, and every new dog starts with a free assessment day so we can find the right group for them. We offer door-to-door pick up and drop off across Berkshire and Buckinghamshire — because we know that's often the piece that makes daycare actually work around your schedule.

02 · Service

Puppy Classes

A 4-week course covering every foundation your puppy needs.

Our puppy classes run on-site for four weeks — enough time to make real progress without overwhelming either of you. We use positive reinforcement to teach basic commands (sit, stay, come, loose-lead walking) alongside essential socialisation skills.

Classes are kept deliberately small (max 6 puppies) so every pup gets individual attention. You'll leave with confident, well-mannered dogs and the vocabulary to keep training at home.

What's included
  • ✓4-week structured course
  • ✓Maximum 6 puppies per class
  • ✓Positive reinforcement only
  • ✓Recap videos after every class
  • ✓Lifetime access to alumni group

1-to-1 Training

Personalised sessions for any behaviour or skill goal.

Every dog has unique needs and personality traits, which is why our one-to-one training is built around you both. We'll start with a conversation, watch your dog, and build a plan — whether that's tackling reactivity, loose-lead work, recall, or advanced skills like agility.

Sessions can be at your home, on one of our fields, or wherever your dog struggles most. Because sometimes the problem only shows up on the high street.

What's included
  • ✓Initial consultation included
  • ✓Your home, our field, or on location
  • ✓Positive reinforcement techniques
  • ✓Written follow-up plan every session
  • ✓Packages of 4 or 8 sessions available
